# Smooth Raccoon: The Slippery Bandit

A top-down stealth-comedy game made for **Summer Beta 2026** — Theme: **Like Butter**

**Team V.O.I.D.** | Jaymes M. · Josh O. · Will Z. · Safia N.

---

## About

Rutabaga the raccoon is convinced the neighborhood is sitting on a gold bar operation. He is wrong. He will never know he is wrong.

Smooth Raccoon: The Slippery Bandit is a top-down stealth game where you sneak through suburban homes, dodge robotic vacuum cleaners (VACooms), and collect what you believe to be gold bars. Each stick of butter you steal makes your movement progressively slipperier — success makes the game harder. The more you take, the harder it is to get out.

Built in **Godot 4.6.3**, playable in browser via **itch.io**.

---

## Gameplay

- **Sneak** through increasingly large homes avoiding VACooms patrol paths
- **Collect** butter (Rutabaga calls them gold bars) as your primary objective
- **Slide** — each butter stick increases ice-physics momentum on your movement
- **Interact** with doors and windows to manipulate patrol routes and find escape paths
- **Escape** with at least one butter to clear the level. Grab them all for 100%.

Getting caught by a VACooms ends the level. Sliding into a wall because you got too greedy is your own fault.

---

## Theme Interpretation

*Like Butter* operates on two levels:

1. **Literal** — the objective is stealing butter
2. **Mechanical** — collecting butter changes how you move; the more you succeed, the slipperier you become

The theme is embedded in the difficulty curve itself.
